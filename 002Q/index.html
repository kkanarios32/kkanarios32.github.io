    Definition. Sub-Gaussian Norm [002P]

    • January 15, 2025
    • Kellen Kanarios

    For \(p > 0\), define \[\lVert X \rVert _{\psi _p} = \inf \left \{K > 0 \mid \mathbb {E} \exp ^{\left (\frac {X}{k}\right )^p}\right \}\] If \(X\) is Sub-Gaussian Random Variables then this is well defined and makes our space a Banach Space.
