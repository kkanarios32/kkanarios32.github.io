Classic denoising is TOO slow for images

Idea: compress images into latent representation space. Then learn diffusion model on latent space. This is the idea in Stable Diffusion

  1. How do we scale? Use Transformers! Diffusion Transformer (DiT)
  2. Still too slow. Enter flow matching.
