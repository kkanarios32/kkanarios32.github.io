What's up with all these fancy optimizers? [0006]

  • October 29, 2024
  • Kellen Kanarios

Throughout my trials and tribulations with experimental RL, I ran into the seemingly prevelant problem of severe instability dependent on optimizers and even hyperparameters of optimizers. In this blog, I aim to understand where and why things have seemingly evolved out of control from trusty SGD.
