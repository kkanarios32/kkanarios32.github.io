Theorem. Hoeffding's Inequality [002N]

  • January 15, 2025
  • Kellen Kanarios

Let \(X_1, \ldots , X_n\) be independent centered Sub-Gaussian Random Variables. Then for any \(a = (a_1, \ldots , a_n) \in \mathbb {R}^n\) and for any \(t > 0\) \[\mathbb {P}\left (\left |\sum _{k = 1}^{n} a_j X_j\right | > t \right ) \leq 2 \exp \left (-c \frac {t^2}{\lVert a\rVert ^2}\right )\]

    Definition. Sub-Gaussian Random Variables [002L]

    • January 15, 2025
    • Kellen Kanarios

    A random variable \(X\) is called \(K\)-Sub-Gaussian if \(\forall t > 0\) \[\mathbb {P}(|X| > t) \leq C\exp \left (-c\left (\frac {t}{k}^2\right )\right )\]
